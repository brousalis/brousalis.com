About

I am a 24 year old software developer living in Brisbane, Australia. You can follow me on Twitter, browse my photos on Flickr, hear what I listen to on Last.fm or check out my code on GitHub. My e-mail address is tate@tatey.com.

My love of computers started when the old man showed me Prince of Persia on a 386. I bounced from a gamer interested in PC hardware, to a Linux fanatic who would install a distribution on anything with 1’s and 0’s before developing a genuine passion for software. Since February 2008 I have been employed as a software developer for Everyday Hero, a service for empowering not for profits with the tools to create richer giving experiences. In December 2010 I graduated with a Bachelor of Information Technology from Griffith University. I have also written about what hardware and software I use.

This Site

I purchased tatey.com in 2003 and kicked it off with MovableType. Since then it's bounced between WordPress, Ruby on Rails and Jekyll. Today, it is automatically generated by Jekyll into a static site. Posts are in Markdown and the source resides on GitHub, never to be lost again! Site is marked up with HTML5 and styled with CSS3. All pages ought to be valid and semantic. If you find an error, please let me know.
